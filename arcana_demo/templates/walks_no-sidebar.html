Tixall Walks

All content, all the time.


There are three public paths in Tixall. A Path Pack with detailed A4 sheets describing each path is available from Dr. A. Andrews, 2, The Hanyards, Tixall, Stafford ST18 OXY, for £1 + Postage. Please make chaeques payable to Tixall & Ingestre Village Hall




Public Footpaths

  • A footpath runs from St Thomas Priory, round the back of Brancote Sewage Works, past the original site of Brancote Farm near the river, and through wood to Holdiford road. Returning along the towpath of the Staffordshire and Worcestershire Canal provides a circular walk.
  • A Bridlepath runs from Bottle Lodge to the White Lodge. It is also possible to access the towpath of the Staffordshire and Worcestershire Canal at Swivel Bridge, to make a more circular walk.
  • A longer Bridlepath runs from Dairy Bridge, past Tixall Pool, across Hanyards Lane and through Upper Hanyards Farmyard, across to Ingestre Park Wall and Fiddlers Lodge, and round part of the County Showground to the A.
